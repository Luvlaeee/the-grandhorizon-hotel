Our Story

A Legacy of Philippine Hospitality

Nestled along the shimmering shores of Manila Bay, The Grand Hotel has been the definitive address for discerning travelers seeking an authentic Philippine luxury experience since 1999.

Every detail — from the hand-woven capiz shell chandeliers to the locally-sourced ingredients in our kitchens — honors the rich cultural tapestry of the Philippines while delivering world-class standards that have earned us Five Star recognition year after year.

Culinary Excellence

A Feast for All Senses

Our celebrated dining experiences honor the extraordinary richness of Filipino cuisine and beyond.

Signature Restaurant
Bahay Kubo
A celebration of Filipino heritage cuisine, reimagined through contemporary techniques using the freshest local ingredients from Benguet to Zamboanga. James Beard-nominated Executive Chef Maria Santos leads our culinary team in crafting unforgettable flavors.
Rooftop Lounge
Langit Bar
Perched 38 floors above Manila Bay, Langit — meaning "sky" in Filipino — is where the golden hour becomes legendary. Craft cocktails infused with calamansi, sampaguita, and rare lambanog spirits await you every evening.
